Engineering Design II


Course Summary: Engineering Design II is a third year STEM course in which students work to apply the skills and knowledge gained during this and previous courses to more advanced products. Some concepts covered during this course are ethical analysis, control systems and fluid power systems, material and mechanical properties, free body diagrams, advanced measurement, and economic analysis. This course builds directly on the two prior to prepare students for the practicum class and for a career in a STEM field.

This class is taught by Mrs. Murphy and is typically taken by students in grade eleven.
